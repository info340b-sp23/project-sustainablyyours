Proposed Application

  1. Who are the users of the application? That is, who will be accessing the website? What impacts might this app have on minority groups?
    • The users of the SustainablyYours.com website are likely to be individuals who are interested in sustainable products and eco-conscious shopping. This can include environmentally conscious consumers, eco-activists, sustainability advocates, and anyone looking to make more informed and responsible purchasing decisions.

    • We will try our best to make sure the website is designed to be accessible to individuals with disabilities, ensuring that it adheres to web accessibility standards and guidelines. This can help ensure that people with visual, hearing, mobility, or cognitive impairments are able to access and use the website effectively.

    • In terms of inclusivity, it is important to consider the representation of minority groups in the products and brands featured on the website. The website should strive to showcase a diverse range of sustainable products from a variety of brands, including those that are owned or operated by minority groups. This can help promote inclusivity and equity in the sustainable marketplace, and support businesses that are owned or operated by underrepresented communities.

  2. What will the users do with the web app? What kind of interactive features are you expecting to provide? Remember that you app will need to include at least "two and a half" significant features; be sure to explicitly identify and explain what all of them are!
    • The main goal of the web app is for our users to learn where and how to buy eco-friendly and ethically sourced products from various online retailers. Given the abundance of resources available, shoppers may have a hard time finding the best sustainable product. Thus, with our app, we aim to let users have an easier and smoother experience buying such products. We want shoppers to come to our website, easily find sustainable products from the site, and help the environment one purchase at a time.

    • Our features for the app will be a “Search and Filter” feature, a “Wishlist” feature, and a “User Accounts” feature. In the search and filter feature, the user will be able to search for sustainable products based on keywords, categories, or other criteria. Additionally, they will be able to refine their search results based on various filters such as product type, brand, price range, eco-certifications, and more. This feature would involve user input to search and filter the products displayed on the website, enabling users to find specific sustainable products that match their preferences.

    • Next, in the wishlist feature, users will be able to create wishlists and add products to their shopping cart for future purchase. This would involve implementing functionality that allows users to save products to their wishlists or add products to their cart, enabling them to keep track of products they are interested in or ready to purchase.

    • Finally, users will be able to create accounts on your website, which would enable them to save their favorite products, create wishlists, and keep track of their purchases. This would involve user registration, login, and profile management features, allowing users to personalize. By creating an account, the user will be able to track their recent activity on the app so that their wishlist, favorite products, and purchase history is tailored to them and their interests.

    • P.S: We also have other features that we want to implement and if any of the features above do not meet the requirements we were thinking of the following other features: Product Rating and Reviews, Social Sharing, Product Comparison, Brand Profiles and are brainstorming more as we go.

  3. What kind of information will the users be viewing? If you're drawing data form an external source (a csv file or an API), include a link and citation to that data.
    • SustainablyYours.com will be able to view various information related to sustainable products and brands, which will be drawn from an external CSV file that contains product data from multiple websites and suppliers that we will obtain from using Web Scraping techniques. Users are going to be able to view:
      • Product Information: Users will be able to view product details such as product name, brand, description, price, product image, and product link to the external websites or suppliers where the products are available for purchase.

      • Sustainability Attributes: Users will be able to view sustainability attributes of the products, such as materials used, certifications, eco-friendly practices, fair trade practices, and other sustainability-related information. This information will help users make informed decisions based on their sustainability preferences.

      • Brand Information: Users will be able to view brand information, such as brand name, mission, sustainability practices, certifications, and impact on the environment and society. This information will provide users with insights into the sustainability practices of the brands behind the products they are interested in.

      • User Reviews: Users will be able to view user-generated reviews and ratings for products. This information can provide valuable feedback from other users' experiences with the products, helping users assess the quality and sustainability of the products.

  4. How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem? That is, how is it a solution to the problem you described?
    • Using SustainablyYours.com will help to solve the problem of creating a single location for sustainable items on one web application as a one-stop destination for sustainable products across various brands where they can be presented with a plethora of curated, eco-friendly, and ethically sourced products. SustainablyYours.com serves as a website connecting people to sustainable products which users can then browse and search through to find items that meet their needs, and be led to the website that they can then purchase the item(s).

    • Additionally, SustainablyYours.com offers comprehensive product information, sustainability attributes, brand information, and user reviews, which empowers users to make informed decisions based on their sustainability preferences. Users can easily compare products and brands based on their sustainability practices, certifications, and impact on the environment and society, enabling them to support brands that align with their values.

    • By providing a platform that promotes sustainability and facilitates informed decision-making, SustainablyYours.com encourages the adoption of sustainable consumption practices, promoting awareness and action towards more sustainable choices. It addresses the problem of fragmented information and lack of accessibility to sustainable products, making it easier for users to incorporate sustainability into their purchasing decisions and contribute to positive environmental and social impacts.

    • The platform aims to connect users with local, sustainable options by showcasing products from local farmers and small businesses that adhere to sustainable practices. This not only promotes sustainable consumption, but also encourages local economies and communities.SustainablyYours.com provides a platform that promotes sustainability across different scales, from global brands to local producers. This supports a more diverse and inclusive sustainable marketplace, providing opportunities for both established and emerging sustainable businesses to thrive and creating a positive impact on the environment, society, and local economies.
